+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**                  | @Applications.Service.ServiceObjects                                                  |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**                    | SetServiceObjectName                                                                  |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**               | ServiceObjectName                                                                     |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**                   | Front-End                                                                             |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**                  | AttributeChanged(Product)                                                             |

| **Priority**                | Normal                                                                                |
