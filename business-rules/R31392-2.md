# R31392-2 Product Price - Set Ship To Customer

+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**                  | @Crm.ProductPrices                                                                    |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**                    | SetShipToCustomer                                                                     |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**               | ShipToCustomer                                                                        |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**                   | Front-End                                                                             |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**                  | AttributeChanged(Customer)                                                            |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**                | Normal                                                                                |

| **Modify**                  | YES                                                                                   |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Applicable Legislations** | [ALL // no condition                                                                  |
