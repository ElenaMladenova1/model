| **Code**             | R29685                                                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**           | @Crm.Pos.Sequences                                                                            |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Name**             | NextNoCannotBeChanged                                                                         |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**        | NextNo                                                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Layer**            | Back-End                                                                                      |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**           | Client Commit                                                                                 |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**         | Normal                                                                                        |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Modify**           | NO                                                                                            |
+----------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Applicable         | [ALL // no condition                                                                          |
