+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

| **Layer**                   | Back-End                                                                              |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**                  | Commit                                                                                |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Record State**            | Inserted                                                                              |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Priority**                | Normal                                                                                |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

| **Message**                 | Object '{DataObject.DisplayText}' cannot be added to favorites because it belongs to an entity that is not allowed to be followed.|
