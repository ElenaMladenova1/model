| **Name**          | ValidateDocumentMakingVoid                                                                       |
+-------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Entity**        | @General.CurrencyDirectories                                                                                |
+-------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
