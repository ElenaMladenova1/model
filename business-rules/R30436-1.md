+--------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

| **Entity**               | @General.Contacts.PartyBankAccounts                                                       |
+--------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

| **Layer**                | Front-End                                                                                 |

+--------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Applicable             | [ALL // no condition needed                                                               |
