+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Attribute**               | SerialNumber                                                                          |

| **Layer**                   | Front-End                                                                             |
+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Events**                  | AttributeChanged(Product)                                                             |

+-----------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
