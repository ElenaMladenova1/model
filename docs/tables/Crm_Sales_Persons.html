    Table Crm_Sales_Persons

    Sales persons (or representatives) are sellers inside the enterprise company who sell the products to customers. Entity: Crm_Sales_Persons

    Owner Tables Hierarchy

    • Cm_Persons
    • Gen_Parties

    Summary

    Name Type Description
    Sales_Person_Id uniqueidentifier PK Unique sales person Id
    Person_Id uniqueidentifier Base personal record
    Enterprise_Company_Id uniqueidentifier
    Commission_Policy_Id uniqueidentifier Current commission policy for the sales person. NULL means there is no commission policy
    Commission_Percent decimal(7, 6) The percentage (0..1) of commission percent. NULL means that there is no commission percent.
    Sales_Person_Group_Id uniqueidentifier The sales person group to which this sales person is assigned.
    Contract_Start_Date datetime The starting date of the contract with the sales person. NULL when it is unknown.
    Contract_End_Date datetime The ending date of the contract with the sales person. NULL when the sales person is still active.
    Enterprise_Company_Location_Id uniqueidentifier The enterprise company location, to which the sales person is assigned. The sales person is allowed to sell to other locations, but this is the default location. NULL means that the sales person is not assigned to any enterprise location.
    Is_Active bit Specifies whether the sales person is active and should be included in the list when choosing sales person through drop-downs, lists, etc.
    Row_Version timestamp
